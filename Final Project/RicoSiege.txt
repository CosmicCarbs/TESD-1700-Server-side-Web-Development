RicoSiege is a mix between Ricochet and Siege which is a 3d stealth game. The Focul point in this game is the fighting Mechanic.
You are very bouncy, being able to leap off any surface you land on, bouncing all over the place. You
are taksed with eliminating all who get in your way of Sieging the Castle. bounce around confusing enemies, 
chain your attacks together for devestating combos and Aim for weak points as you pass by.
