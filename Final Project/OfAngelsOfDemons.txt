Of Angels/Of Demons is actually two games, Whichever you play determines what side you are on. You are a college student
who got tied up in an unliley scenario, every 1000 years or so Angels Fall and Demons Rise and the Metropolis becomes an 
Apocoliptic Battle Ground. You were caught up in the catastrophe and are enlisted to help in this devestaing war due to
being chosen by a mask maker and receiving a Mask of Resonance (either angel or demon form). The decieding facor of this 
war is to obtain all 7 masks of Heavenly Virtue/Deadly Sin. The mask will change based on who curretnly has it either 
Angel form or Demon form. Will you be able to turn the tide of this war or will an antagonist stand against you and win?
